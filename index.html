Rolling Stock Planning System

Implemented a system for NKMZ to forecast rolling stock requirements (rail wagons) for product shipments. The solution enabled the sales department to specify planned wagon needs, production to update planned and actual production dates, and the railway department to place timely wagon orders. The system also recorded shipment confirmations and captured delivery data. As a result, wagon planning was streamlined, manual coordination reduced, and the right number of wagons ensured at the right time.
